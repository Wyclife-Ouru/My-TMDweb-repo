Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to our most common questions about orders, shipping, and products.

Ordering & Payments

1. What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), PayPal, and Apple Pay. All transactions are securely processed.

2. Can I modify or cancel my order after placing it?

We process orders quickly! If you need to modify or cancel, please contact us within 2 hours of placing your order using our contact form or calling us at +254 123 456 78. Once an order is shipped, we cannot make changes.

Shipping & Delivery

3. How long does shipping take?

Shipping times depend on your location and the shipping method chosen at checkout. Standard domestic shipping typically takes 3-7 business days. International shipping can take 7-21 business days.

4. Do you ship internationally?

Yes, we offer worldwide shipping! Please note that international customers are responsible for any customs duties, taxes, or brokerage fees incurred upon delivery.

Returns & Exchanges

5. What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee on all products, provided they are unused and in their original packaging. For returns, please visit our dedicated Returns Portal linked in your order confirmation email.

Still have questions?

If you couldn't find the answer you were looking for, don't hesitate to reach out to our support team.
