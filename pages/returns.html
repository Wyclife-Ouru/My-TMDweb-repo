How to Start a Return (Step-by-Step)

Follow these simple steps to successfully return your item(s).

Step 1: Initiate Online

Visit our Returns Portal with your order number and email address to start the process.

Step 2: Package & Ship

Securely package the item(s) in the original packaging, if possible. Affix the prepaid shipping label we provide.

Step 3: Receive Refund

Once received and inspected, refunds are processed back to your original payment method within 5-10 business days.

Detailed Policy Terms

Return Eligibility

  • Items must be returned within 30 days of delivery.
  • Items must be unused, unwashed, and in the same condition that you received them.
  • Items must be in the original packaging with all tags attached.
  • Sale items or gift cards are non-refundable.

Refund Process

  • Once your return is received and inspected, we will send you an email notification.
  • If approved, your refund will be processed, and a credit will automatically be applied to your original method of payment.
  • Processing Time:Refunds take approximately 5-10 business days to appear on your statement.
  • Original shipping costs are non-refundable unless the return is due to an error on our part (e.g., damaged or incorrect item).

Exchanges

If you need to exchange an item for a different size or color, please indicate this on the Returns Portal. We offer free shipping on the new exchange order. We will ship the replacement item once the original item is received and inspected.
