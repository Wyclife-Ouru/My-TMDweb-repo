Privacy Policy

Effective Date: November 21, 2024

Your privacy is of utmost importance to The Morning Dew Store. This policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you visit our website or make a purchase.

1. Information We Collect

1.1. Personal Information

We collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when registering, placing an order, subscribing to marketing, or contacting us. This information may include:

  • Name, billing address, shipping address.
  • Email address and phone number.
  • Payment information (which is processed securely by third-party processors).

1.2. Usage and Technical Data

When you visit our website, we automatically collect certain information about your device and browsing activity, including:

  • IP address and browser type.
  • Operating system and device identifiers.
  • Referring source, pages viewed, and time spent on pages.
  • Clickstream data (how you navigate the site).

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for various business purposes, including:

  • To fulfill and manage orders: Processing payments, shipping goods, and providing customer support.
  • To communicate with you: Sending order confirmations, updates, and responding to inquiries.
  • For marketing and promotional purposes: Sending emails about new products, special offers, and events, based on your preference settings.
  • To improve our website: Analyzing usage data to enhance user experience, website performance, and product offerings.
  • To ensure security: Protecting our site against fraud and ensuring the integrity of our systems.

3. Sharing Your Information

We only share your information with third parties in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service Providers: We share data with vendors, consultants, and other third-party service providers who perform services for us (e.g., payment processing, shipping carriers, email delivery). These parties are bound by confidentiality agreements.
  • Legal Compliance: We may disclose your information where legally required to do so, such as in response to a subpoena or court order.
  • Business Transfers: In connection with, or during negotiations of, any merger, sale of company assets, financing, or acquisition of all or a portion of our business by another company.

4. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies (like web beacons) to access or store information. A cookie is a small data file stored on your computer or mobile device. We use cookies for essential functions (e.g., maintaining your shopping cart) and for analytics (e.g., understanding traffic patterns). You can configure your browser to reject cookies, but this may affect your ability to use certain features of the website.

5. Data Security

We use organizational and technical security measures designed to protect the security of any personal information we process. However, despite our safeguards and efforts to secure your information, no electronic transmission over the Internet or information storage technology can be guaranteed to be 100% secure. We encourage you to use secure passwords and not share them with anyone.

6. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • The right to request access to the personal data we hold about you.
  • The right to request correction of inaccurate data.
  • The right to request deletion of your personal data.
  • The right to opt-out of marketing communications.

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the details provided below.
